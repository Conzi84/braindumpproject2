9:T684,<h1>Intraday Futures Trading Notes</h1>
<h2>The Market I Trade</h2>
<p><strong>ES (S&#x26;P 500 E-mini)</strong> and <strong>NQ (Nasdaq 100 E-mini)</strong></p>
<p>Liquid, volatile, perfect for 1-4 hour windows when I can focus.</p>
<h2>What I've Learned</h2>
<h3>Time of Day Matters</h3>
<p><strong>9:30-10:30 AM ET</strong> → Highest volatility, most opportunities</p>
<ul>
<li>Market opens, algorithms hunt stops</li>
<li>News gets priced in</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>11:00 AM-2:00 PM</strong> → Choppy, avoid</p>
<ul>
<li>Low volume, fake breakouts</li>
<li>Better to step away</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>2:30-4:00 PM</strong> → Second window</p>
<ul>
<li>Institutional positioning for close</li>
<li>Cleaner trends</li>
</ul>
<h3>Position Sizing</h3>
<p>Never risk more than 1% of account per trade. Period.</p>
<p>With ES at $50/point, that means tight stops. Forces discipline.</p>
<h3>Patterns That Repeat</h3>
<ol>
<li><strong>Opening range breakout</strong> → Wait 15 minutes, then trade the break</li>
<li><strong>Failed breakout</strong> → Fade the move, tight stop</li>
<li><strong>VWAP retest</strong> → Price respects VWAP like gravity</li>
</ol>
<h3>What Doesn't Work</h3>
<ul>
<li>Revenge trading after a loss (learned this the hard way)</li>
<li>Trading during Fed announcements (too random)</li>
<li>Ignoring risk management "just this once"</li>
</ul>
<h2>Integration with AI</h2>
<p>Currently testing: Can Claude analyze my trade journal and spot patterns I miss?</p>
<p>Early results: Yes. It caught that I win more on Tuesday/Thursday. No idea why yet.</p>

<p>This note evolves. Every trading session adds data.</p>
